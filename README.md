# Akage's Bloodbath Collection
This is a collection of usermaps meant for Blood (1997) focusing on both Bloodbath and Teams gametypes.

Rather than a dump of maps found over on the internet like many collections do, these are handpicked levels that I believe are fun to play in Bloodbath.

As of 2026, You can play NotBlood multiplayer with people over at [NukemNet](https://nukemnet.com/home). Join their [Discord](https://discord.gg/AmJXc8PuNS) to catch game activities.
